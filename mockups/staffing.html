§ 02 — What Makes Our Staff Different

Hired for personality.
Trained for the brief.

Every ambassador is interviewed in person, reference-checked, and rated on previous activations. We maintain an active roster — not a Craigslist spreadsheet — and we cut people who don't meet the bar.

Before they ever meet your consumer, they've been briefed on the brand voice, the talking points, and the escalation protocol. They arrive in uniform. They arrive on time. They leave the site cleaner than they found it.

§ 03 — The Roster

Six roles. One standard.

01 Frontline

Brand Ambassadors

The face of your activation. Product pitching, sampling, guest engagement, lead capture. Warm, on-brand, unshakeable under pressure.

02 Leadership

Event Captains

Your on-site producer. Runs the crew, manages time, handles agency comms, and makes the judgment calls your brief didn't cover.

03 Sampling

Sampling & Demo

Product-trained specialists for food, beverage, CPG, and tech demos. Food-handling certified where it matters.

04 Festival

Festival & Concert Crews

High-energy environments, long days, big crowds. Crews built for Stampede, concerts, and multi-day activations.

05 Language

Bilingual Talent

French, Mandarin, Cantonese, Punjabi, Tagalog, Spanish. Western Canada isn't one audience — your talent shouldn't be either.

06 Specialty

Specialty Talent

Bartenders, stylists, mixologists, on-camera talent, sign-language interpreters. If the brief is unusual, we have a call sheet.
